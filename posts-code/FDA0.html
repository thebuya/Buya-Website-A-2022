Factory-DAO Proposal


Alongside an incredible team, I am excited (on your behalf), to introduce a new way of working together : a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) that's focused on bringing Africa up to speed with the rest of the world! We're calling it the FactoryDAO, and it's a system that lets us make decisions together in a more collaborative and trustworthy way.

Here's how it works: we use an ERC20 token to govern the ecosystem through voting on the blockchain. This means that there's no single leader or oligarch in charge - we all have a say!
Plus, we've created a native cryptocurrency that can be used for transactions within the ecosystem, and even African capitalists can accept payments in this currency.

All discussions and decisions are made on our forum at Syndicate.org ,and any disputes are handled at the Aragon Decentralized Dispute Resolution platform. And here's the really cool part - our community is made up of experts in all sorts of fields, from trading analysts to blockchain lawyers. We even have guilds for these different specialties, so we can all work together to make this ecosystem even better!

We vote annually to elect a Council of signatories to the community's multisignature wallet. Anyone can be a Council member, as long as they have a good reputation and online presence. And the best part? We take the power back and give it to the people. So join us in this exciting new adventure, where we work together to create smart factories, agricultural transformation centers, and on and off-chain startups. Let's take Africa to new heights!
